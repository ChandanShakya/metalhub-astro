We stand behind the quality of our products. If something isn't right, here's how we'll make it right.

## Eligibility for Refund

| Condition | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Time window | Within **7 days** of delivery |
| Product condition | Unused, with original packaging and accessories |
| Proof required | Order confirmation or receipt |

## How to Request a Refund

1. **Contact us** on WhatsApp at **+977-9860301608** with your order details
2. **Share photos or video** of the product and explain the reason for return
3. **Our team reviews** your request and provides return instructions
4. **Return the item** — we'll arrange pickup from your address
5. **Inspection** — once received, we inspect within 24 hours
6. **Refund processed** — within **1–4 business days**

## Refund Methods

| Method | Processing Time |
|--------|----------------|
| eSewa / Khalti | Instant |
| Bank Transfer | 1–2 business days |
| Store Credit | Instant |

**Note:** Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a product defect or our error.

## Exchanges

Want a different product? Contact us to arrange an exchange. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If your product arrives damaged or defective:

- Notify us within **7 days** of receiving your order
- Provide photos showing the damage
- We'll send a **replacement or full refund**, including shipping costs

## Non-Refundable Items

The following are **not eligible** for refund or exchange:

- Products that have been used, washed, or damaged by you
- Items without original packaging
- Custom or personalized orders
- Change of mind
- International orders
- Bulk purchases (unless damaged due to our error)

## Warranty

All products come with a **7-day warranty** covering manufacturing defects from the date of delivery. This warranty does not cover:

- Damage from misuse or improper care
- Unauthorized modifications
- Normal wear and tear
