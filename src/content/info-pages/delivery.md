We work with reliable courier partners to get your order to you safely, whether you're in Kathmandu or across the globe.

## Processing Time

| Order Type | Processing Time |
|------------|-----------------|
| Small packages | Within **1 business day** |
| Bulk orders | **7–15 business days** |

*Processing may take longer during festivals or peak seasons.*

## Delivery Timeframes

| Zone | Standard Shipping | Express Shipping |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| Inside Kathmandu Valley | **1–2 days** | Within 2 hours |
| Outside Valley | **2–5 days** | Not available |
| International | **10–20 days** | Not available |

## Shipping Charges

| Zone | Rate |
|------|------|
| Kathmandu Valley | **Free** |
| Outside Valley | NPR 100–300 (weight-based) |
| Express (Valley only) | Additional charges apply |
| International | Calculated at checkout |

**Note:** Express and international orders require full payment in advance.

## Order Tracking

- **Domestic orders:** You'll receive a confirmation SMS with tracking details once your order is dispatched.
- **International orders:** Tracking number is provided within 2–4 days after the courier issues it.

## International Orders

For international shipments:

1. Add desired products to cart
2. Send us the cart screenshot on WhatsApp
3. Share your full name, phone, email, and complete shipping address
4. We'll send you final pricing with shipping estimates
5. Complete full prepayment to confirm your order
6. We'll pack and dispatch via our partner courier
7. Tracking number shared via WhatsApp once available

**Important:** Customs duties, import taxes, and brokerage fees are the responsibility of the recipient. We are not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance.

## Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your order is lost or arrives damaged:

- Contact us within **7 days** of delivery
- Provide your order number and photos of the damage
- We'll arrange a replacement or full refund

**Note:** Exchange or refund is not available for change of mind.

## Incorrect Address

Please double-check your shipping details at checkout. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.
