We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

## Return Window

You have **7 days** from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

## Conditions for Return

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Time frame | Within 7 days of delivery |
| Product condition | Unused, unwashed, undamaged |
| Packaging | Original packaging with all tags and accessories |
| Proof | Order confirmation or receipt |

## How to Return

1. **Contact us** on WhatsApp at **+977-9860301608**
2. **Share your order number** and reason for return with photos
3. **We'll arrange pickup** from your address at no extra cost
4. **Once received**, we inspect the item within 24 hours
5. **Refund is processed** within 1–4 business days

## What Can't Be Returned

- Products that have been used, worn, or damaged by you
- Items without original packaging or tags
- Custom or personalized orders
- Gift cards

## Damaged on Arrival?

If your product arrives damaged:

- Take photos immediately
- Contact us within **7 days**
- We'll send a **free replacement** or **full refund**
